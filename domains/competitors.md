## Local Competitors

### Bayse (bayse.markets)
**Status:** Live | **Strongest direct competitor**
**Company:** Trence Technologies, Inc.
**Contact:** compliance@bayse.markets

**Product:**
- Largest Nigerian prediction market by market catalogue: 73 markets (snapshot 2026-05-30)
- Categories: Sports 30/73 (41%), Politics 25, daily X post-count markets (7), entertainment
- Binary YES/NO only — no multi-outcome parimutuel
- Pricing: cents-based (share costs X cents, pays $1 if correct) — radically cleaner than percentage/multiplier format
- BYUSD stablecoin (native), plus Naira wallet
- Head-to-head (H2H) challenges and Fantasy Premier League (FPL) — Kasiro has neither
- Native iOS and Android apps — Kasiro is web-only
- 18.7K followers on X

**What Bayse does well:**
- Deepest local market catalogue (12+ categories)
- Hyper-local content: X post count predictions, local surgery fundraisers, Nollywood feuds
- Three-column desktop grid communicates volume and depth immediately
- Cents-based pricing is the clearest UX pattern in the local market
- Has H2H and FPL as additional product lines

**What Bayse does not have:**
- Multi-outcome parimutuel — this is Kasiro's structural differentiator
- Africa Watch strip or continent-level framing

**Bayse legal/policy reference docs** (saved in OneDrive Predicto as competitive reference):
- Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Trading Prohibition, FAQ, Help Center — all Bayse originals, useful as drafting templates for Kasiro's own legal docs

**Monitor Bayse for:** market categories, question framing, UI patterns, operator pricing decisions, new product lines

### 2Sabi (2sabiapp.com + 2sabi.org)
**Status:** Live | **Second local competitor**
**Company:** TOO SABI LIMITED (RC 8837676)
**Licence:** Osun State Government of Nigeria — only licensed prediction market in Nigeria
**Infrastructure:** Enterprise-grade, US-hosted

**Product (two separate products):**

**2sabi main app (2sabiapp.com):**
- House model — 2Sabi is the counterparty, not a peer-to-peer pool
- Traditional decimal odds (1.25x, 1.32x, 1.56x) — familiar to sports bettors
- ₦ Naira denominated
- Categories: Sports, Entertainment, Politics, Crypto, Religion
- Creator content markets: Sabinus/Layi Wasabi/BrainJotter skit predictions
- Ticket marketplace — users trade existing positions
- Daily-recurring entertainment markets (most performant category)
- 35 markets live as of 2026-05-30

**2Sabi Hub (2sabi.org):**
- Crypto & economy specialist product
- Covers: Bitcoin halving targets, XRP outcomes, NGX index, eNaira adoption, Fed rate decisions
- Live crypto price direction markets (5-minute windows)
- Daily bonuses on active bets, cashback when bets don't win
- Also on decimal odds model

**What 2Sabi does well:**
- Only licensed player — regulatory moat
- Guaranteed liquidity (house model) solves cold-start
- Daily-recurring entertainment markets build weekly habits
- Fixture-bundling (award category bundles, 8-market awards bundle)
- Real-time crypto price markets

**What 2Sabi doesn't have:**
- AMM continuous pricing
- Multi-outcome parimutuel
- Africa Watch framing

**2Sabi live board gaps vs Kasiro (2026-05-30):**
- Kasiro politics-heavy (6/8 markets = 75%); 2Sabi entertainment-heavy — Kasiro inverted from what works
- 2Sabi top categories: daily-recurring entertainment, fixture bundles, awards bundles

**Monitor 2Sabi for:** audience positioning, market types they choose not to cover, award/entertainment bundling patterns

### Wysemarket (app.wysemarket.com)
**Status:** LIVE (was described as pre-launch in April 2026 design critique — now confirmed live)**
**Company:** wysemarket LLC

**Product:**
- ₦ Naira denominated
- Multi-outcome parimutuel display (shows % chance per outcome) — closest to Kasiro's model
- Markets: AMVCA categories, UCL player performance, Naira PMS price, national grid collapse, Apple Music Nigeria chart, Asake album debut
- Very small pool sizes (₦0 – ₦55K per market) — early stage
- Bottom tab navigation (Markets / My trades / Wallet / Profile)
- Editorial design: massive black type on cream-yellow with spray-paint texture — premium visual identity

**What Wysemarket does well:**
- Multi-outcome display — shows percentage chance per outcome, cleanest UX for parimutuel
- African-relevant market topics (grid collapse, PMS price, AMVCA)
- Strong visual identity (most premium-looking of local competitors)

**What Wysemarket doesn't have:**
- Volume (₦0–₦55K per market vs Kasiro's larger pools)
- AMM binary markets
- Any meaningful liquidity

**Monitor Wysemarket for:** parimutuel UX patterns, visual identity evolution, market topics

## Global Reference Platforms (pricing benchmarks, trending signals)

### Polymarket (polymarket.com)
**Status:** Largest global prediction market by volume
**Model:** Decentralised CLOB, USDC, Polygon smart contracts, UMA oracle
**Categories:** Politics, crypto, economics, culture, sports
**Volume:** $8M+ per top market

**Use for:** Implied probability benchmarks on international politics, crypto, macro events. When localising a global event, check Polymarket price first.

**Not for:** African-specific markets (they don't cover these)

---

### Jupiter (jup.ag/prediction)
**Status:** Live on Solana
**Model:** Decentralised, SOL-based

**Use for:** Global trending event signals and category gaps that can be localised for African audiences. Check what's trending on Jupiter before a market scan session.

---

### Kalshi (kalshi.com)
**Status:** CFTC-regulated US market
**Model:** Centralised, USD fiat, official government data sources
**Categories:** Economics (CPI, NFP, Fed decisions), elections, sports, crypto

**Use for:** Economic indicator pricing models (inflation, FX rate band construction) and resolution methodology best practices. Kalshi's resolution criteria are the industry gold standard for economic markets.

---

### Ultramarkets (app.ultramarkets.xyz)
**Status:** Live
**Model:** Margin layer — LONG/SHORT positions with 2x–10x leverage on prediction markets
**Categories:** Elections, sports, crypto, economics, tech
**Volume:** $1M–$10M per market

**Strategic note:** Adds leverage as a product angle. Not a priority for Kasiro now (regulatory risk) but worth tracking as a future product direction.

---

### Luno (luno.com)
**Status:** Live — 10M+ users across Africa
**Product:** Crypto exchange + Price Predict feature (predict crypto price for prizes)
**Note:** Biggest player by user base in African crypto. Price Predict is a side feature, not a full prediction market. Different product entirely but shares the same target user.

## Key Competitive Intel

- Bayse and 2Sabi are both politics-underweight; Kasiro is inverted (politics-heavy) — fix content mix
- WC 2026 started June 11; Wysemarket and 2Sabi had WC markets; Kasiro launched R32 batch June 2026
- 2Sabi uses cents-based pricing UX — evaluate whether Kasiro should adopt this display format
- 60M sports bettors in Nigeria are the real competition (not just prediction markets) — conversion from bettor to trader is the primary acquisition story
- Bayse flagged as potentially illegal (no licence); 2Sabi is only licensed player
